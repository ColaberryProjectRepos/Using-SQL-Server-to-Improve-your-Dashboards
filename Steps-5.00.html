Retention Rate

This is a Video of Ali calculating retention rate over time.  The calculations were completed in SQL to make the Dashboard reporting easier.  This is a great example of using your SQL Skills to enhance your Reporting skills.


Customer Retention Rate Formula

Retention Rate = ((E-N)/S) x 100.   

E = Number of customers at end of the period. 

N = Number of customers acquired during the period. 

S = Number of customers at the start of the period.
